Proof Stack Core

Projects upload encrypted treasury snapshots. Smart contracts validate balances against public ledgers and mint a luminous proven badge straight onto PulseX listings. No spreadsheets, no waiting on centralized auditors.

Attestation Workflow

  • Stake PLS as collateral and mint a zk proof capsule that binds wallet holdings + identity hashes.
  • Route the capsule through ProveX oracles; governance-approved templates handle institutions vs. anon whales.
  • Receive a portable credential NFT that any PulseChain dApp can verify in under a block.
  • Cash in compliance benefits: accelerated onboarding, pre-cleared OTC desks, curated PulseX spotlight slots.
